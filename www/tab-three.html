Kubernetes Services expose an application to other applications in your cluster, or to users on the Internet. Services provide load balancing across one or more Pods. As with Replication Controllers the set of Pods is identified by a label selector query. 
apiVersion: v1
kind: Service
metadata:
  name: nginx-service
spec: 
  ports:
    # the port that this service serves on
    - port: 80
  # the query that identifies Pods that provide this Service
  selector: 
    app: nginx
  # 'LoadBalancer' services are exposed to the external Internet. 
  type: LoadBalancer
